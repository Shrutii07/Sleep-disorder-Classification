CAP Disease Classification

The positive patients can be classified into one of the following diseases:

  • INS : Insomnia
  • NFLE : Nocturnal Frontal-Lobe Epilepsy
  • NARCO : Narcolepsy
  • RBD : REM sleep Behaviour Disorder
  • PLM : Periodic Leg Movement
